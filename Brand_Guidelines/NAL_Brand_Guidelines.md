## 01 — Brand Foundation

### Brand Purpose
To equip mid and senior level leaders with brain science-backed tools to lead with clarity, confidence, and speed — especially under pressure and disruption.

### Brand Promise
When leaders engage with this brand, they should feel trusted, inspired, and excited to grow — knowing the guidance is rooted in real science, not buzzwords.

### Target Audience
Mid to senior level leaders in organizations who are navigating change, managing disruption, and looking for practical, evidence-based solutions to perform under pressure.

### Brand Personality

| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| **Intelligence** | Grounded in neuroscience and research |
| **Scientific** | Evidence-based, credible, rigorous |
| **Practical** | Actionable, clear, immediately useful |
| **Human-Centered** | Warm, accessible, people-first |

### Three Emotional Goals
Every brand touchpoint should make the audience feel:
1. **Trusted** — Jason is a credible expert with real science behind him
2. **Inspired** — They can grow, change, and lead better
3. **Excited** — Learning this is going to be practical and worth their time

### Color Usage Rules

**On Dark Backgrounds (Navy Deep / Navy Mid):**
- Primary text → White
- Headings and accents → Gold
- Secondary text → Slate
- Never use Gold Dark on dark backgrounds (fails contrast)

**On Light Backgrounds (White / Off White):**
- Headings → Navy Deep
- Body text → Slate Dark
- Links and text accents → Gold Dark
- Buttons and large graphic accents → Gold

**Accessibility:**
- Navy Deep + White = AAA ✓
- Navy Deep + Gold = AA Large ✓
- Never use Gold text on White at small body text sizes — use Gold Dark instead
- Always test contrast before publishing

## 03 — Typography

### Primary Typeface: Manrope
- **Source:** [Google Fonts — Manrope](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Manrope)
- **Weights in use:** 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold)
- **Avoid:** Weights below 400 in digital contexts

### Type Scale

| Style | Size | Weight | Line Height | Tracking | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / H1** | 52px | 800 | 1.05 | −1px | Hero headlines, book title |
| **H2** | 36px | 700 | 1.15 | −0.5px | Section titles |
| **H3** | 24px | 700 | 1.25 | 0 | Sub-section headers |
| **H4** | 18px | 600 | 1.35 | 0 | Card titles, feature names |
| **Lead / Intro** | 18px | 400 | 1.65 | 0 | Intro paragraphs, pull quotes |
| **Body** | 16px | 400 | 1.75 | 0 | All body copy |
| **Small / UI** | 14px | 500 | 1.5 | 0 | Buttons, nav, UI labels |
| **Caption** | 12px | 500 | 1.5 | 0 | Captions, footnotes, meta info |
| **Eyebrow Label** | 11px | 700 | — | +0.14em | Section labels, category tags (UPPERCASE) |

### Typography Rules
- Eyebrow labels are always UPPERCASE with generous letter-spacing
- Never mix typefaces — Manrope is the only brand font
- Minimum body text size: 14px for digital, 11pt for print
- Pull quotes: 24px / 700 / Gold Dark color on light, Gold on dark

## 04 — Logo & Wordmark

### Wordmark Construction
The wordmark is set in **Manrope ExtraBold (800)**. The "**N**" and "**A**" in NeuroAdaptive are always rendered in **Gold (#F5A623)**. All other characters follow the background rules below.

```
NeuroAdaptive
Leadership
Jason Jones, Ph.D.
```
*Where N and A = Gold, all other text = White (dark bg) or Navy Deep (light bg)*

### Approved Wordmark Versions

| Version | Background | Text Color | N & A Color |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Navy Deep `#0A1628` | White | Gold `#F5A623` |
| **Light** | White / Off White | Navy Deep | Gold `#F5A623` |
| **Compact** | Any | Navy Deep or White | Gold `#F5A623` |
| **Reverse** | Gold `#F5A623` | Navy Deep | Navy Deep |

### Icon Mark
A standalone **"N"** set in Manrope 800, used for:
- Favicons (16×16, 32×32)
- Social profile avatars (circle crop)
- App icons
- Small spaces where full wordmark won't fit

**Icon Mark Specs:**
- Character: **N**
- Font: Manrope 800
- On Gold background → N in Navy Deep
- On Navy background → N in Gold
- Corner radius: 16px (standard), 50% (circle/social)

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "N" character on all four sides of the wordmark.

### Wordmark Do's
- Always keep the "N" and "A" in Gold across all versions
- Use the full wordmark (NeuroAdaptive Leadership) as the primary logo
- Scale proportionally — never stretch or squish
- Use only the approved color combinations listed above
- Use the icon mark for favicon, profile pictures, and app icons

### Wordmark Don'ts
- Never change the font from Manrope ExtraBold
- Never use Gold text on white for small wordmark sizes
- Never add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the wordmark
- Never change the "N" or "A" color to anything other than Gold
- Never place the wordmark on a busy or patterned background without a dark overlay
- Never abbreviate to "NAL" as a substitute for the full wordmark

## 06 — Brand Voice & Tone

The NeuroAdaptive Leadership brand speaks like a trusted expert who also happens to be a great communicator. Smart and grounded, but never stuffy. Practical and direct, but never cold.

### Voice Traits

| Trait | What It Means | What to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Credible** | Back every claim with science, but explain it so anyone can understand | Arrogant, jargon-heavy, or over-academic |
| **Inspiring** | Challenge leaders to think differently and grow, with warmth | Empty hype or hollow motivation |
| **Direct** | Say what you mean, clearly and concisely. No fluff | Blunt, dismissive, or cold |
| **Practical** | Every idea comes with a "so what" — always give people something to do | Abstract, theoretical, or purely conceptual |
| **Human** | Talk like a smart, caring colleague — not a textbook | Clinical, robotic, or impersonal |
| **Curious** | Lead with questions as often as answers | Know-it-all, prescriptive, or preachy |

### Words & Phrases We Use
upgrade · thrive · clarity · adapt · brain science · practical · proven · resilient · human-centered · performance · disruption · confidence · leaders like you · wire your brain · real-world · tools · growth · evidence-based · NeuroAdaptive

### Words & Phrases We Avoid
synergy · paradigm shift · disruptive innovation · leverage · ideate · bandwidth · circle back · neuroplasticity (as jargon without explanation) · thought leader · guru · secret · hack · "studies show" (vague attribution) · game-changing · revolutionary

### Tone Adjustments by Channel

| Channel | Tone |
|---|---|
| **Website hero** | Bold, confident, punchy — big statements |
| **Blog / Articles** | Conversational expert — smart but accessible |
| **Email** | Direct, personal, like a colleague reaching out |
| **Social media** | Punchy, curious, thought-provoking |
| **Speaking bio** | Credible, warm, achievement-focused |
| **Book resources** | Clear, practical, step-by-step |

## 07 — Visual Language

### Core Visual Motifs

**Neural Dot Grid**
A subtle repeating dot grid pattern referencing neural networks. Use as a background texture on dark sections at low opacity (10–15%). Never use on light backgrounds.

**Gold Rule Lines**
Thin horizontal lines in Gold (`#F5A623`) used to separate sections and create visual hierarchy. Maximum weight: 2px. Minimum length: 40px.

**Gold Accent Sections**
Use Gold backgrounds (`#F5A623`) sparingly for callout boxes and pull quotes only. Never use for full page sections or large areas. Text on Gold backgrounds must be Navy Deep.

**Circular / Radial Forms**
Concentric circles and radial patterns referencing neural networks and brain activity. Use in illustrations, section backgrounds, and decorative elements.

**Data Visualization**
All charts and graphs use Navy and Gold only. Clean, minimal styling with Manrope labels. No 3D effects, gradients, or unnecessary decoration.

**Numbered Step Blocks**
Large numbered blocks (Gold-accented borders, Navy background) used to break down frameworks and processes. Clear, scannable, and action-oriented.

### Photography & Imagery Direction

**Use:**
- Real leaders in real environments — boardrooms, conference stages, team meetings
- Authentic imagery, not staged stock photos
- Diverse representation across industries and backgrounds
- Neural network renders and brain imagery with dark backgrounds and gold/amber lighting (matching book cover aesthetic)
- Jason Jones: professional, confident, and approachable headshots on dark or neutral backgrounds

**Avoid:**
- Clip art or generic stock images of people pointing at whiteboards
- Neon or purple tech imagery (this is not our aesthetic)
- Overly corporate cold imagery
- Anything that looks AI-generated or generic
- Bright, saturated backgrounds that fight with the brand palette
