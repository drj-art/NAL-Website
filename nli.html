Six dimensions

The science of leading under pressure — measured.

Each dimension maps to a different brain system that drives how leaders perform when stakes are high. Your NLI tells you which ones are working for you, and which to strengthen first.

01

Clarity

How well your prefrontal cortex holds the signal when noise spikes — your ability to think clearly when others can't.

02

Confidence

The grounded steadiness that lets you make and own a call. Tracks reactivity, second-guessing, and threat sensitivity.

03

Speed

How quickly you move from input to decision to action. Where reward circuitry helps — and where it hijacks you.

04

Regulation

Your ability to notice and shift your state in real time, before pressure leaks into your words and choices.

05

Adaptability

How well you reframe, recover, and re-engage when the plan changes. The core NeuroAdaptive muscle.

06

Culture-shaping

How the brains around you respond to yours. Measures the signals you send your team — intended or not.
